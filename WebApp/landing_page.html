Welcome to ParlaStats!

Welcome to ParlaStats a statistical tool made for analysis of parliamentary discourse
from multiple (not only) European countries. This tool is built on top of the comparable corpora
of the ParlaMint project with an extention for czech data utilizing the ParlaMint-based
improved corpus ParCzech. It was build (and is being built) as a bachelor thesis
at UFAL MFF supervised by doc. Mgr. Barbora Vidov치 Hladk치, Ph.D. and Bc. Maty치코 Kopp.

Current capabilities

As of current state of the art, the application can work with the earlier mentioned improved czech corpus
ParCzech, Slovenian and Ukrainian ParlaMint corpora.

You can query these datasets using the application's querying interface accessible
under the "Tools" tab in the left part of the navigation bar. There, you will be presented with a form in which you can specify
what are you looking for, what conditions should the results satisfy, how should they be grouped and ordered.
After constructing and sending the Query you can see the results visualized as table, that allows for exporting the data in
"excel-like" CSV format. In addition to that, all results are visualized into a bar (primary) and a line chart accessible under the "GRAPH" tab.
The graph allows for zooming in and out, and option of excluding certain data by clicking them at the legend of the graph at the top.
